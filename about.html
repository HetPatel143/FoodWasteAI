About FoodWaste-AI

Reducing food waste. Fighting hunger. Empowering communities with technology.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between surplus food and hunger by connecting donors with NGOs and food banks, using the power of AI and community action.

Our Vision

A world where no good food goes to waste and every person has access to nutritious meals.

Our Values

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships between donors, NGOs, and communities to create lasting change.

Sustainability

Our platform is designed to reduce food waste and environmental impact, one meal at a time.

Impact

We measure success by the food saved, people fed, and CO₂ reduced through our network.

Innovation

We use smart technology and data to make food rescue faster, safer, and more effective.
