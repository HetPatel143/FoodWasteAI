How FoodWaste-AI Works

Connecting surplus food to those in need, powered by smart AI and a caring community.

For Donors

1. Register

Create your donor account in seconds.

2. List Surplus Food

Enter details, quantity, and pickup location.

3. Get Matched

Our AI finds the best NGO for your donation.

4. Confirm Pickup

Coordinate with the NGO for a smooth handoff.

5. See Your Impact

Track food saved and lives helped in your dashboard.

For NGOs

1. Register

Sign up as an NGO and set your preferences.

2. Get Notified

Receive instant alerts for new donations nearby.

3. Accept & Schedule

Review, accept, and arrange pickup with the donor.

4. Distribute Food

Deliver food to those in need and update status.

5. Report Impact

Share outcomes and stories with the community.

How AI Matching Works

Our AI considers food type, quantity, expiry, and location to match donations with the most suitable NGOs, ensuring quick and safe delivery. The more you use the platform, the smarter the matching becomes!

  • Real-time matching for urgent needs
  • Location-based optimization
  • Safety and compliance checks
  • Continuous learning for better results
